Stock By Location

Optimize your inventory control with powerful multi-view reporting and export capabilities

Overview

Stock By Locations Balance | Multi-company Stocks Balance

This module enhances stock management by offering real-time inventory tracking and detailed insights across multiple locations, ensuring streamlined and accurate inventory control. With interactive pivot, tree, and graph views, it's easy to analyze inventory for multiple companies. Integrated with purchase and sales orders, and enhanced visibility in product variant management.

Key Features

Multi-View Reporting

Analyze inventory with pivot, tree, and graph views for in-depth stock insights.

Customizable Reports

Generate detailed PDF and XLSX reports tailored to your business needs.

Purchase/Sales Stock By Location

Track and manage purchase and sales stock levels by location.

Multi-Company Support

Manage inventory across multiple companies with ease and efficiency.

Configuration

After installing the module, the users that they have access to the group Inventory / Stock By Locations Report can access to the report,
and that group inherited by Inventory / Administrator which mean all the users in it have access to the report.

Pivot View

Interactive pivot view for inventory analysis, you can analyze with it the: Qty on hand, Free, Reserved, Forecast, Incoming, Outgoing.
by Companies, locations, categories, products, Product variants.

Analysis Multi Company

You can analyze stocks of multiple companies by selecting them, which means that the user can analyze stocks based on the companies he has access to.

Graph View

Graph view for visual representation of inventory.

PDF/XLSX Report

You can print customized report as you wish by choosing:

  • Report Format
  • Companies (as per selected companies)
  • Locations
  • Measures
